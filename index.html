About Me

I am a doctoral candidate at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, and my current research interests include causal discovery, deep learning, and embedded hardware circuits. I will share my doctoral research work and some interesting content on causal discovery and deep learning on this platform.

Research

Causal Discovery

Causal Discovery​ focuses on identifying cause-and-effect relationships from observational data to uncover the underlying mechanisms of complex systems.

Time Series Learning

​Time Series Learning​ involves developing models to analyze and predict sequential data by capturing temporal patterns and dependencies.

Complex Network

Complex Network​ studies the structure, dynamics, and behaviors of interconnected systems to understand their properties and resilience.
