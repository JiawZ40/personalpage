About Me

I am a doctoral candidate at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, focusing on causal discovery, deep learning, and embedded hardware circuits.

Research Areas

Causal Discovery

Investigating cause-and-effect relationships in complex systems using advanced statistical methods and machine learning techniques.

Time Series Learning

Developing innovative approaches for analyzing temporal data patterns and making accurate predictions in dynamic systems.

Complex Networks

Studying the structure and dynamics of interconnected systems to understand their behavior and resilience.
